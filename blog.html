The Moon!!

A person who don't like the moon doesn't exist, do they? It reminds me of many memories from my childhood which most of the people of this generation are missing. The moon is my first date. Yes I used to have my dinner watching the moon from my terrace. The moon is my first teacher. Yes it taught me how to spread light in the darkest. The moon is my happiness. Yes it showers lots of love..
